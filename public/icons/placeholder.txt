
You need to create the following icon sizes for your PWA:
- icon-72x72.png
- icon-96x96.png 
- icon-128x128.png
- icon-144x144.png
- icon-152x152.png
- icon-192x192.png
- icon-384x384.png
- icon-512x512.png

These should be square PNG images with a transparent background featuring your Zodible logo.
You can use a tool like Figma, Photoshop, or online converters to create these from your existing logo.
